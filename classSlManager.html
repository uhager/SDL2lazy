Moves a SlRenderItem's position in the renderQueue_ to before of after the target item.
beforeOrAfter is 0 for before, 1 for after. Larger (smaller) values will result in the target item begin inserted further upstream (downstream) of the target. (Handle with care, currently no test for valid iterators beyond +1...)
