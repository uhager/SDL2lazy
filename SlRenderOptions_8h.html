enum SlRenderOptions

specifies where, what, and how to render.

Specifies whether to use the SlTexture's source or destination SDL_Rect. Also specifies use of colour mod and alpha mod.
Currently implemented:
sourceRect to clip part of the texture
destinationRect to specify where and how big to render
colour mod (SDL_SetTextureColorMod)
alpha mod (SDL_SetTextureAlphaMod)
